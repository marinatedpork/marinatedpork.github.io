Hello. I'm Matt.

I am from Saint Paul, MN, and am currently living in Chicago, IL. I am an aspiring full stack developer. I figured I might as well complete the lifestyle, so I joined DevBootcamp. Before the summer of 2014, I was less than novice - I was that guy who thought it was a huge deal to implement a responsive email template. Nevertheless, I always looked at coding as something I would learn how to do someday. Now learning comes by the ton, and I am soaking up all that I can.

Right now, I have a working knowledge of the following languages:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • jQuery
  • JavaScript
  • Ruby
  • SQL

I invite you to take a click around the website, and read a blog or two. I'd like to think that there's at least one good nugget of information, or even entertainment, for everyone. Thanks!

-Matt, September 14, 2014
