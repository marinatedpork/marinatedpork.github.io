WHO WE ARE
HOW WE WORK
BACK

Northwoods Revolution is a Chicago-based commercial production company. We specialize in digital cinematography and editing, and are a one-stop-shop capable of taking a project from inception to final delivery.

We are experienced in working with a wide variety of clients, such as Leo Burnett, Optimum Nutrition, Red Robin, and many more. We are efficient, easy to work with, and hold ourselves to the highest standards of production, all while maintaining competitive rates.

We are available to travel for most projects. Don't hesitate to contact us with an out-of-state or out-of-country proposal.

POSTPRODUCTION

POSTPRODUCTION

All the smoke and mirrors

Your project is assembled quickly and efficiently and a rough first draft is sent to you for your consideration. You send back notes, and we do whatever those notes tell us to do. This process can take anywhere from one or two drafts to ten drafts. After all this is done, we deliver the product and raw footage in the pre-determined form, you pay us, and everyone is happy!
