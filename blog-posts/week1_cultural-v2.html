The Culture of Dev Bootcamp

July 16, 2014

The DBC culture appears to be one that I may flourish in. I am a very interactive person that loves talking things through with others and helping out when I can. I am very excited to get into the classroom and do some peer pairing in real life.


My first impression of DBC is that it strikes a balance between relaxed and focused. There is this certain air of work hard play hard that I am picking up on.


I see myself fitting in well with this culture. After speaking with a number of the other folks in my cohort, I am quite excited to get into the classroom.


My expectations have not changed. What makes me nervous is staff members speaking so much about asking people withdraw or holding them back. I don't want to be a straggler!
